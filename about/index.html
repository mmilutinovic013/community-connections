Serve Your Local Community

Student experience

We developed a website to allow people in a community to easily access all local community service opportunities in one spot so that they may be more involved in such events. Users navigate our map feature to find local events in their area and attend. They can manage all their events in the calendar to stay up to date and organized. Our sites does exactly what our vision outlines, it uses the power of the internet to connect anyone in the community to all volunteer events near them, fostering community and connecting their region.
