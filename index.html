Come Serve Your Local Community!

Our Mission

Community Connections is a project that we began, which is a platform that connects people to their own community. By aggregating local volunteer opportunities and making them available to the public. Our vision and hope is to lift the entire community to a higher level of social responsibility and foster an atmosphere of volunteerism and comradery. We want to increase visibility of volunteer opportunities and increase student engagement in volunteer opportunities and volunteer organizations.
